Engagement Process

What files are required?

We primarily work with .3dm (Rhino), .rvt (Revit), and .dwg files. High-resolution PDFs of plans and elevations are also helpful for double-checking fine details.

What is the typical lead time?

Lead times vary by complexity. Concept massing models typically take 2 weeks, while highly detailed marketing or presentation models can take 4-8 weeks depending on the level of detail required.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide secure, custom-crated shipping worldwide. We use white-glove delivery partners to ensure your model arrives in perfect condition, regardless of its destination.

How do I request a quote?

Simply navigate to our Contact page and provide your project details, including scale, preferred materials, and any deadline constraints. We will provide a detailed proposal within 48 hours.
